## Course context

This project was produced for <strong>PPOL 5204 · Big Data Analytics for Public Policy</strong> at Georgetown University's <strong>McCourt School of Public Policy</strong>, Spring 2026. The assignment frames the work as a pitch to a grant funder or policy board — teams explain why their policy question matters, present their analysis, and propose a path forward for larger-scale investigation.
